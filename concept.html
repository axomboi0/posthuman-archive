Transhumanism

Transhumanism is a philosophical movement that advocates the use of science and technology to enhance human intellectual, physical, and psychological capacities. Topics include artificial intelligence, genetic engineering, life extension, and mind uploading.

Extropianism

Extropianism promotes continuous self-improvement, technological progress, and expansion beyond biological limits.

Singularitarianism

Belief that artificial intelligence may reach a point where intelligence surpasses human control.

Cosmism

Idea that humanity should expand beyond Earth and overcome biological limits through science.

Rationalism

Focus on logic, probability, AI risk, and long-term future planning.

Effective Altruism

Use of reason and evidence to do the most good possible, often focusing on global risk and future civilization.

Longtermism

Belief that the long-term future of humanity is extremely important.
