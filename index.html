TESCREAL

Tescreal is a term used to describe a group of related philosophical movements including transhumanism, effective altruism, rationalism, and longtermism. These ideologies focus on artificial intelligence, human enhancement, existential risk, and the long-term future of civilization. Source: Wikipedia

Transhumanism

Transhumanism is a philosophical movement that advocates the use of science and technology to enhance human intellectual, physical, and psychological capacities. Topics include: Artificial intelligence Genetic engineering Mind uploading Life extension Post-human evolution Source: Wikipedia
