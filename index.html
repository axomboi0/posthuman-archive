About Tescreal

Tescreal is a term used to describe a group of related philosophical and ideological movements including Transhumanism, Effective Altruism, Rationalism, Longtermism, and technological futurism. These movements focus on the future of intelligence, artificial general intelligence, human enhancement, and the survival of civilization. Source: Wikipedia
